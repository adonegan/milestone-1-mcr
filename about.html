My Chemical Romance is an American rock band from Newark, New Jersey. They formed in 2001 and were top of their game until 2013 when they disbanded. Band members: lead vocablist Gerard Way, guitarists Ray Toro and Frank Iero, bassist Mikey Way, and Bob Bryar on drums.

I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love, in 2002. Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge, in 2004. The Black Parade, in 2006. Danger Days: The True Lives of the Dangerous Killjoys, in 2009.
